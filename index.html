Asbestos

Our comprehensive asbestos testing and remediation services provide expert identification, safe removal, and thorough cleanup of asbestos-containing materials.

  • Surveys and Inspections
    • Expert residential asbestos detection and regulatory guidance for safe handling and removal
      • Mandatory for all contractor-led renovations or demolitions, regardless of building age
  • Post-abatement Clearance Testing
    • Post-abatement air quality testing to confirm complete asbestos removal and ensure regulatory compliance
      • Mandated by OSHA for all employers with employees exposed to asbestos environments

Mold

Our in-depth mold testing and remediation services identify, assess, and safely eliminate mold growth, ensuring a healthy indoor environment for both homes and businesses.

  • Inspections and Assessments
    • Expert mold detection and analysis using advanced air and surface sampling techniques
    • Mandatory when visible mold growth meets and/or exceeds 25 contiguous square feet
  • Protocols
    • Customized mold remediation strategies for both homes and businesses, ensuring thorough and safe remediation
  • Air Quality and Clearance Testing
    • Post-remediation verification testing: Comprehensive air and surface sampling to ensure complete mold eradication

Lead

Our expert lead testing and remediation services safeguard your home or business by identifying, assessing, and safely removing lead hazards.

  • Inspections and Risk Assessments
    • Expert lead detection and risk assessment for pre-1978 residential and commercial properties, including:
      • Mandatory testing for contractor-led renovations in Texas
      • Comprehensive consulting on lead management strategies
  • Gun Range Exposure Assessments
    • OSHA-mandated annual lead exposure monitoring: protecting employee health through rigorous workplace lead dust testing
